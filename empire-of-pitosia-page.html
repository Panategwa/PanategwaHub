Calendar System

The Empire of Pitosia uses a numeric calendar system based on the birth of Pitos. The year is divided into 11 months, each with 8 days, followed by a 5-day festival period at the end of the year. Months are referred to by number, starting from Month 0 (the first month) up to Month 11 (the last). Example: 2400 years, 3 months, and 5 days after the birth of Pitos would be written as 2400|3|5.
