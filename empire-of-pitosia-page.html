Government

The Empire of Pitosia is ruled by Monarch Pitos, who has absolute control over laws, military, and foreign policy.

  • Absolute monarchy led by Monarch Pitos.
  • Centralized decision-making.
  • Focus on stability and prosperity.

Society

Pitosian society values discipline, loyalty, and productivity. No official religion exists, and education is highly encouraged.

  • No religion — purely secular state.
  • Education is mandatory for all children.
  • Quality of life tied to loyalty.

Economy

The Pitosian economy is highly industrialized, producing advanced technology, weapons, and luxury goods.

  • Industrial and technological powerhouse.
  • Exports luxury goods and weaponry.
  • Trade is heavily regulated.
