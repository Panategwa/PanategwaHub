The Pitosians

Physical Appearance

  • Height: Average around 2.1 meters (about 6'10"), taller due to lower gravity
  • Skin Color and Texture: Bronze to deep olive, with a faint leathery texture to protect from wind and cold
  • Hair: Thick and dark (black or deep brown), providing insulation during cold seasons; grays with age
  • Eyes: Larger than humans, adapted for low light with colors like gray-gold, icy blue, or hazel
  • Other Features: Slim noses that warm cold air, smaller ears to prevent heat loss, and 6 sturdy, long fingers for tool use

Culture & Society

  • Clothing: Layered, durable robes and cloaks designed for stormy, windy weather — often in earthy tones like grays, blues, and blacks
  • Architecture: Low, wide buildings with thick walls to resist earthquakes and storms
  • Diet: Omnivorous — mixed diet of hardy plants and storm-resistant livestock, with preserved foods like smoked meats and root vegetables
  • Technology Level: Medieval-inspired with advanced metallurgy and weather-resistant gear
  • Military: Skilled in fighting in harsh weather conditions, using spears, swords, and battle animals with heavy layered armor

Environmental Adaptations

  • Skin: Thick epidermis for protection against wind and cold
  • Eyes: Extra transparent eyelids to shield against storms and dust
  • Respiration: Nostrils can narrow to block out dust and cold air
  • Balance & Strength: Wide stance and strong core muscles to withstand high winds and rough terrain
