The Rashoots

Physical Appearance

  • Height: Average around 1.9–2 meters, slightly leaner build
  • Skin Color and Texture: Light Amber, slightly rough to handle sun and sand exposure
  • Hair: Dark or sandy tones, thinner than Pitosians to stay cool
  • Eyes: Radius of ~3cm, adapted for bright sunlight; colors include amber, gold, or light brown
  • Other Features: Slim noses and smaller ears, 5 fingers, long limbs for better heat regulation

Culture & Society

  • Clothing: Lightweight robes in neutral tones, suited for warm and dry regions
  • Architecture: Simple, low buildings with thick walls to stay cool during the day
  • Diet: Omnivorous — crops and animals adapted to drier climates
  • Technology Level: Medieval-inspired, focus on survival in hotter regions
  • Military: Skilled in mobility and endurance in warm, open terrains

Environmental Adaptations

  • Skin: Slightly thicker to prevent sun damage
  • Eyes: Protective eyelids to reduce glare
  • Respiration: Nostrils can narrow to keep out dust
  • Heat Management: Lean build and long limbs help dissipate heat
