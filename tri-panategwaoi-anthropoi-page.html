Tri-Panategwaoi Anthropoi
(Tri-Panats)

Physical Appearance

  • Size & Weight: Infant: ~5 kg, 60 cm; Juvenile: 40–60 kg, ~1.5 m; Adult: ~120 kg, ~2.5 m
  • Height: Adults around 2.5 meters (8 ft)
  • Skin Color & Texture: Variable, depending on regional adaptations
  • Hair: Varies by region; can be thick or sparse
  • Eyes: Adapted to light conditions in environment; colors vary
  • Other Features: Six fingers, highly dexterous; physical traits vary by environment

Culture & Society

  • Overview: Dominant sentient species of Panategwa d, highly adaptable and technologically advanced
  • Population: ~14 Billion
  • Habitat: Tropical rainforests, arid deserts, mountains, and arctic zones
  • Diet: Omnivorous; meats, fruits, vegetables, grains
  • Reproduction: Viviparous, ~6-month gestation, usually 2–3 offspring per birth
  • Lifespan: Average of ~650 years, leaders of civilizations can live up to thousands of years
  • Races: Pitosians — largest cultural and political influence; plus minor regional variations
  • Society & Culture: Highly organized civilizations with urban centers, trade, military structures, and strong emphasis on knowledge, technology, and art
  • Behavior: Social and cooperative; nomadic or settled depending on environment

Environmental Adaptations

  • Adaptable to almost all regions of Panategwa d
  • Physical traits and body heat regulation adjust to local climate
  • Dexterous and strong, capable of surviving harsh and diverse terrains
