Tri-Panategwaoi Anthropoi
(Tri-Panats)

Overview:
The dominant sentient species of Panategwa d, highly adaptable and technologically advanced.
Habitat:
Almost all regions of Panategwa d — tropical rainforests, arid deserts, mountains, and arctic zones.
Population:
~14 Billion
Size & Weight:
Infant: ~5 kg (11 lbs), ~60 cm (23.6 in)
Juvenile: 40-60 kg (88-132 lbs), ~1.5 m (5 ft)
Adult: ~120 kg (265 lbs), ~2.5 m (8 ft)
Diet:
Omnivorous; primarily meats but also fruits, vegetables, grains, and other available resources.
Reproduction:
Viviparous, with a gestation period of ~6 months. Usually two or three offspring per birth.
Life Span:
Average ~650 years; elders often live isolated or as advisors.
Races:
Pitosians — largest cultural and political influence, plus minor regional variations.
